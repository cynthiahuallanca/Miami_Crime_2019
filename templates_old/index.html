Miami Dade Crime Study (2015 to current) - June 2019

by Yissel Fernandez, Cynthia Huallanca, Emily Mo and Carolina Muizzi


Miami Dade, Florida is the largest county in Florida. It is home to 2.7 million people. It is the nation's fourth largest school district. It's the seventh largest metropolitan area in the USA. Miami is the fourth hottest city and the seventh rainiest city in the country.

Facts about Miami Dade :

  • 51.45 % female and 48.55 % male
  • Adults over 25 : roughly one fifth has not received a high school diploma
  • About 54% foreign born
  • about 29% speak Spanish only
  • about 27% English-speaking individuals
  • 20% under 18 years of age
  • 16% over 65 years old
  • Median household income : $46,338
  • Poverty : 16.7%
